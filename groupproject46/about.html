Hadi was born and raised in beautiful city of Shiraz, Iran, home of the 13 century poet Hafez. He is a self-taught artist showing interest in drawing from early age, illustrating scenes and drawing family portraits in pen and pencil. At age 13, the walls of his parents’ house were plastered with portrait drawings of family members, celebrities and world famous individuals. As he began reading Hafez’s poetry, he developed an interest in Persian style miniature painting because Hafez’s poetry publications are always accompanied by Persian style miniature illustrations.

In 1976, Hadi migrated to America to study engineering and it was not long before his passion for art became the casualty of the political and social events of late 70’s and early 80’s. From thereafter, for a period of 33 years he did not paint.

In mid-2014, a friend of him, Johane Amirault who is an outstanding artist herself, encouraged him to join her in her studio. There was no paper or pastel available in this studio, so he found himself with no choice but to work with brush, paint and canvas.
