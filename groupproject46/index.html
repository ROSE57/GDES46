    Sealife Digital painting

"My past painting experience is with pastel, now I paint primarily with acrylic on canvas with diverse subjects and variety of styles. The subjects of my paintings are from what once was and what may not be; the things we have overlooked in the past or fail to see at this very moment. I paint to cling fondly to the past and to cherish what is all around us today - people, nature, objects.

I have recently been experimenting with digital Painting; one mouse click at a time. I still start with the traditional way, sketching on paper, scanning them into computer and begining the painting proiess. At the moment, my digital works are mostly the illustrations of Persian Sufi poetry."
